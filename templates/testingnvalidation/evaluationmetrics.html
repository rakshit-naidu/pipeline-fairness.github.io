TESTING & VALIDATION : EVALUATION METRICS

The processes by which a model is determined to be performing well, both in relation to other models in the training set, but also on unseen data. Common sources of bias here include metric underspecification or misspecification (not including notions of bias or including inappropriate notions of bias for example) as well as using improper data for testing or validation (typically that is not representative of the deployment scenario).
