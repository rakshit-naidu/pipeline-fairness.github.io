PROBLEM FORMULATION : PREDICTION TARGET

Translating the complex policy or business problem into a predictive problem. There has been a string of work enumerating fairness problems that arise from problem formulation, particularly through connecting to the social science concept of validity. Some problems of bias that may come up here include: (1) The choice of a biased proxy for a prediction target (e.g. re-arrest or re-conviction for a recidivism prediction model, since whether or not someone committed a crime cannot reliably be observed). (2) The use of non-causally related or otherwise arbitrary factors in the prediction task.
